## Descriptive Statistics - Air Quality

@fig-airquality explores the impact of temperature on ozone[^1] level...

[^1]: Ozone is a molecule composed of three oxygen atoms.

```{r}
#| label: fig-airquality
#| fig-cap: "Temperature and ozone level."
#| echo: true

ggplot(airquality, aes(Temp, Ozone)) + 
  geom_point() + 
  geom_smooth(method = "loess")
```


@fig-ozone shows the key statistics measures and an outline of central tendency, spread, and skewness of ozone level.

```{r}

#| fig-cap: "Ozone level."
#| echo: false
```
